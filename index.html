AP Bizzle Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 80-20 Ground Beef
  • Kosher Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ancho Chili Powder
  • MSG
  • Brioche Buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Sriracha
  • Pickles
  • (optional) Any other burger topping

Instructions

  1. Acquire the Beef

    2. For the beef you want to get an 80-20 beef. I have personally had the best luck with beefs that look like the one below


  2. Lay Out Sheets of Parchment Paper, and cut out a sqaure the size of a large spatula you have
  3. Weigh out balls of beef that weigh 2-4oz and place them on the parchment paper. I recommend more patties with cheese on each and stack them up 2x. After you place them on the parchment, take your spatula and put the cut out piece of parchment between it and the meat. Press down hard until you have a flattened piece of meat
  4. I recommend seasoning with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and (optional) chili powder.
  5. Cut out the square with the flattened beef on it and place it to the side and repeat and make all the patties you need
  6. Heat up your cast iron pan, or grill. You want it to be pretty hot (medium-high or so)
  7. Throw the burgers on the grill. You want the bottom to get real crispy fast without cooking it through too much. Once you flip the burger just throw cheese on it and give it a short while to cook on the other side.
  8. Throw your buns inside facing on the ground with a little bit of butter and toast it a lil bit.
  9. Lastly server up on a brioche bun with some ketchup and mayo, (optional sriracha)
